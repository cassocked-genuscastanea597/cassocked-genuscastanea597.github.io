## 📋 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, look at these solutions first.

*   Taskbars do not appear: Check your display driver version. Restart the application to refresh the connection to the monitors.
*   Wallpaper does not stretch: Go to the Wallpaper tab and verify that the Mode is set to Stretch, Span, or Fill.
*   Software fails to open: Ensure you have the latest version of the .NET framework installed on Windows.
*   Window moving does not work: Check your keyboard shortcut settings in the Hotkeys tab to see if the keys conflict with other running apps.

If these steps do not fix your problem, try restarting your computer to clear out active processes that might occupy your display ports. Remove any other software that modifies your desktop to prevent conflicts.
